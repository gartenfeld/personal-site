Why this site?

There must be, somewhere out there, people who care deeply about the science of language, who have made the non-trivial decision to start a technology company to explore their curiosities. I want to find and get to know these people, and if we are heading the same direction, we can build a company together.

These pages explain my opinions about "What kind of company to build" (Ideology) and "Why it can actually work" (Canvas). If you see something that sparks your interest, or if you know someone who might want to see it, please get in touch.
